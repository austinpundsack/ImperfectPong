Instructions

Welcome to (Im)Perfect Pong. Pong is a game consisting of two paddles and a ball. The objective of the game is to get the ball into the opponent's goal, while using your paddle to keep it out of your own. Player 1 (on the left) controls their paddle with W and S, while Player 2 (on the right) controls their paddle with the up and down arrow keys. Once a point is scored, the ball is reset to the middle of the screen after a brief delay. After a few points, the players will be given a choice of cards that will modify the game in some way. Click the buttons below to either return to the main menu or begin the game. Have Fun!
