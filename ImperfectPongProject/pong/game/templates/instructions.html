Pong Game Instructions

Welcome to the Pong game! Game's interface consists of two paddles and a ball. The ball deflects off the paddle upon impact. You need to obtain 11 points when your opponent misses the ball to win. Additionally, the game can either be played by two people or one against a computer-controlled paddle.

  • Use the up and down arrow keys to move your paddle.
  • The game ends when one player scores 11 points.
  • If the ball hits the top or bottom of the screen, it will bounce back.
  • If the ball hits the left or right side of the screen, the opposite player scores a point.
  • The game will automatically reset when one player scores a point.
